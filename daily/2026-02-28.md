The most significant trend in this digest is the emergence of terminal-based AI agents as the primary interface for AI-assisted development. Unlike browser-based tools, these agents:

1. **Have Full System Access** - They can read files, execute commands, and interact with development environments directly
2. **Integrate with Existing Workflows** - Developers don't need to switch contexts
3. **Enable Agentic Behaviors** - They can perform multi-step tasks autonomously
